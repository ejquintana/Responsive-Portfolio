About Me

I'm passionate about living and experiencing life to the fullest. Whether storm boarding - stand-up paddle boarding in big waves with heavy winds/snow/whatever, skiing gnarly steeps, trying to solve complex problems for clients, or helping those in need, my desire is to leave this world a better place than when I found it.


My other passion is my family. We laugh, love, cry, and try to build each other up and make home a place to try and fail with style... Whether on skiing adventures, bike rides, or whatever, we are happiest together. We are at our happiest out on the trails, biking, paddle boarding, watching movies, or gaming.
